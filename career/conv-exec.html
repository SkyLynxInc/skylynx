Conversion Executive
Overview: Converting new registered customers and driving overall sales and profitability in alignment with marketing objectives.
Duties & Responsibilities
  • Leverage existing tools to reach new registered customers and successfully convert them to start buying/using the product/services
  • Be proactive to meet the personal KPIs in terms of member conversion as required in conjunction with department targets
  • Handle customer queries via communication channels e.g. social media, Messenger programs, e-mail, and phone calls
  • Focus on Customer Service quality while approaching potential customers, tailor standard service to customer expectation
  • Escalating all issues related customer accounts to Team Leader and follow up the case completely, including getting feedback from each case and keep record
  • Follow up and complete daily tasks according to schedule planned
  • Be frank, friendly, and flexible to customers in communication style
  • Taking necessary actions regarding customer queries during the shift, including hand over the cases to the next shift or other departments
Requirements
  • Must possess a Bachelor's/College Degree in Marketing or any related course
  • Minimum 1-2 years working experience in related field
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Strong team player
  • Outgoing personality with a 'can do' attitude
